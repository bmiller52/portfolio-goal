Things That I do

I mainly work in Blender. Here are some of the things I'm good at.

  • Modeling

    This is the first step you take when starting a scene. It's probably the easiest and least time consuming.

  • Texturing

    Like many other 3D designers, I have been switching over to PBR materials. It adds a whole new level of detail with Normal, Gloss, Reflecion, and Displacement maps.

  • Lighting

    I've been using HDR skies for the past year now. This makes the scene realistic to the real world with a 360 degree photo.

  • Compositing

    This is my favorite stage of completing a render. I have been experimenting with the after effects and have taken my renders to the next level.
